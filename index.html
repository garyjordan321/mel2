Welcome to the Fur Ball 2025: The "Dogfather"

On April 25th, 2025, all funds raised on this spectacular evening will support the Clinical Services and work done by the Cheyenne Animal Shelter.

Your support and attendance at the Fur Ball ensures that our mission to expand generosity, practice empathy, and promote kindness across our community continues now and well into the future. Join us for this fun evening featuring adoptable pets, heart-warming stories, exciting auction items and experiences, and more.

Reserve your spot today by clicking the button below to show your support of the Cheyenne Animal Shelter Clinical Services!

Featured Adoptable Pet

Meet Q! This sweet little senior guy is looking for the pawfect retirement home to call his own. At 12 years old, Q would love to join a family that will appreciate all his fluff the right way. Q would prefer a home with older kiddos who can give him space when he needs it and, due to his love adventure, will need a yard with a good, sturdy fence! This grinning guy loves treats and would make a wonderful addition to your movie marathons on the couch. Come by the Cheyenne Animal Shelter and meet Q today!
